Book my Show

Bigtree Entertainment's BookMyShow is India's largest entertainment ticketing website. Headquartered in Mumbai, it is the only destination for movie and non-movie options like events, plays and sports.

Book my Show was started in August 1999

Bigtree Entertainment

Amid the raging coronavirus disease (Covid-19) pandemic, online movie and events ticketing brand BookMyShow has laid off as many as 200 employees, informed Ashish Hemrajani, the chief executive officer (CEO) and co-founder of BookMyShow's parent company Bigtree Entertainment.

he firm is present in over 650 towns and cities in India and works with partners across the industry to provide unmatched entertainment experiences to millions of customers. Over the years, the company has evolved from a purely online ticketing platform for movies across 6,000 screens to end-to-end management of live entertainment events including Music, live performances, theatricals, sports, and more, all accomplished at par with global standards.
